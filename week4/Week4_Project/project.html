Drink of the Month

The Black Unicorn

Introducing The Black Unicorn! This Vodka based drink is sure to give you the poise and feistiness of the gracious Libra. The Black Unicorn is methodically sweetened with Grenadine and splashed with the flavor of Rum. Like a Libra, the Vodka is balanced with a smooth texture that sophistically enriches the flavors and darkens the beverage, exuding a dark yet sweet illustration. Like a Libra, this diplomatic ebony, red drink impersonates the eloquence and graciousnes of wine but can be very dangerous when taken lightly.

Featured Drink

The Yaya Rita

This drink is a personal favorite. The Yaya Rita is named after yours truly. This margarita is based on the powerful and energizing qualities of Hennessey cognac. The Yaya Rita is a show-starter and demands to be the center of attention. This margarita lightens up your taste buds and consumes you with a burst of sweet flavors and enriching textures. The Yaya Rita is nurturing and caring, until you consume all of what it has to offer. Like a cancer the the Yaya Rita is persuasive and cunning. This margarita seems easy and stable, then it will send you on your own emotional rollercoaster.

The Minion

Loyal, whimsical, warm-hearted, and reliable. This may sound like the traits of a Virgo, but what is being described are the qualities of this month's newest addition, The Minion. This summer's sweet tea is admired by and based on the variety of smooth textures that are cemented in Whiskey. The Minion's mild temperament is sizzled by the bold taste and carbonation of Ginger Ale. The Minon seems simple and easy to handle until it amuses your underestimation. Like a Virgo, the Minion sweet tea is patient and enjoys waiting....waiting until you feel comfortable, then it strikes.
